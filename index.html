FAQs

Is it really unlimited?

Yes — candidates, job postings, applications, and users are unlimited on both plans. Reasonable fair‑use applies to automation and API usage.

Can we invite the whole team?

Absolutely. Add hiring managers, recruiters, and interviewers — there are no seat caps.

What does Pro include?

Everything in Go, plus organizations (Staff & Corporate HR), interview management, recruitment cycle, downloads, reporting & dashboards.

Can we export our data?

Yes — CSV downloads and reports are available on Pro. MVP includes standard exports.

Refunds & cancellations

Pro is billed annually. You can cancel anytime to stop auto‑renewal; access continues until the end of your current term. We don’t offer partial refunds for the remaining period.
